PVC GREEN SUCTION HOSE


1. Material: Polyvinyl Chloride

The material of PVC Green Suction Hose is Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC). It is typically made of extruded soft plastic,
often reinforced with a rigid helix, providing flexibility and good abrasion resistance.

2. APPLICATION :It Has various applications

Some of them are:
  1. Water Supply in Agriculture and Irrigation.
  2. Water Supply in Agriculture and Irrigation.
  3. Suction of Sewage disposal, Saline Solution, Light acids, and other chemicals (except solvents).
  4. Chemical applications.
  5. Water Irrigation.
  6. Construction, water transfer, agricultural, and industrial applications.

3. Purpose:Facilitate the transfer of fluids in various applications

Theased on the provided information, the purpose of a PVC green suction hose is to facilitate the transfer of
fluids in various applications. These hoses are strong, lightweight, and flexible, making them suitable for construction,
water transfer, agricultural, and industrial uses. The smooth bore PVC construction minimizes friction loss, allowing for
full production flow. Additionally, PVC suction hoses are flexible and have good abrasion resistance due to their extruded
soft plastic construction reinforced with a rigid helix. They are commonly used with water pumps, trash pumps, and centrifugal
water pumps in construction sites and other settings.
