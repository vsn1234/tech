SS CORRUGATED HOSE


1. Material:Stainless Steel

The stainless steel grades commonly used in corrugated hoses include 304, 304L, 321, 316L, and 316Ti.
These hoses are known for their flexibility, durability, and ability to handle various types of fluids,
gases, or materials in different applications.

2. APPLICATION : Industrial, commercial, and domestic applications

Some Common Application are

  1. IRON & STEEL MANUFACTRING

    These hoses are used in the transportation of molten metals, slag, and other materials in steel plants.

  2. Oil and Gas industry:

    SS corrugated hoses are used for handling oil, gas, and other petrochemical products in refineries and other facilities.

  3. AUTO COMPANIES:

    SS corrugated hoses are used in the automotive industry for various applications, including exhaust systems and fluid transfer.

3. Purpose:

QUCIK RELEASE COUPLING HAS VARIOUS PURPOSE SUCH AS The primary purpose of SS (Stainless Steel) corrugated hose is to provide a flexible and durable conduit for the transfer of
various substances, such as gases, fluids, and steam, in industries like gas, chemical, and steam. The corrugated design allows
for flexibility while maintaining pressure tightness, and the stainless steel construction ensures resistance to corrosion and
high temperatures. Additionally, the external layer of stainless steel wire braid provides extra strength and prevents axial elongation
when pressure is applied.
