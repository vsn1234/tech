SS FLEXIBLE HOSE


1. Material: STAINLESS STEEL

The material of SS flexible hoses is stainless steel (SS). These hoses are manufactured from special
stainless steel sheet strips, with common types being 304/304L and 316/316L, as per TSE and EN ISO European standards.
They are also lightweight, highly resistant, extremely flexible, and have a wide operating temperature range, from -200°C to +800°C.
Stainless steel hoses have a high melting point, making them resistant to fire and suitable for various applications.

2. Application: Industrial applications

Following are the application of SS FLEXIBLE HOSE
  1. SS flexible hoses are widely used in various industrial applications due to their flexibility, high strength, and resistance to corrosion.
  2. They are particularly suitable for applications where high temperatures and harsh operating conditions are prevalent.
  3. SS flexible hoses are used in combis and cookers in houses, meters at the entrances of flats, fountains, armatures, and decorative indoor gas installations.
  4. They find application in food processing, chemical, pharmaceutical, and cosmetics industries.

3. Purpose:

The purpose of a flexible hose is to carry gases and fluids, such as chemicals, water, steam, and air, from one location to another.
They are designed to be practical and can be used for various applications across different industries. Flexible hoses offer several
advantages over rigid pipes and tubing, such as being simple to route, withstanding vibration, and accommodating thermal expansion or
contraction. This makes them suitable for situations where there is movement of one end of the conductor while the opposite end is fixed,
or when the system needs to handle vibrations or temperature changes.
