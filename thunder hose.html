THUNDER HOSE


1. Material:Ultra High Molecular Weight (UHMW) Polyethylene

In summary, the materials used in the Thunder hoses mentioned in the context are silicone/metal tubing and ultra high
molecular weight (UHMW) polyethylene.

2. Application: TRANSPORTATION OF MATERIALS

The application of Thunder Hose includes:
  1. Delivery and suction hose for solid, gaseous, and liquid media.
  2. Use with vacuum pumps, agricultural machinery, and the construction industry.
  3. Sewer cleaning and groundwater drainage.
  4. Non-toxic hose suitable for transferring food, beverages, and refreshing drinks.
  5. Suction and delivery line for lubricating oil, petrol, and petroleum-based products.

3. Purpose: Securely hold and connect two pipes or tubes together

The purpose of a PP (Polypropylene) clamp is to securely hold and connect two pipes or tubes together, ensuring a leak-proof and
torque-free seal. PP clamps are commonly used in various industries, including food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical processing,
where maintaining a sanitary and hygienic environment is crucial.
