PU DUCT HOSE


1. Material: POLYURATHANE

The material of PU Duct Hose is polyurethane (PU). This flexible ducting is often used for industrial air movement,
fume control, and dust collection applications. The polyurethane wall and spring steel wire helix reinforcement provide
excellent compressibility, abrasion resistance, and chemical resistance. The thinner wall allows for a high compression rate,
lower weight per meter, and great flexibility

2. APPLICATION : SERVAL APPLICATIONS LIKE:

PU duct hoses, made of flexible polyurethane (PU), have several applications due to their lightweight, durable, and flexible nature.
They are commonly used for light-duty air, dust, and material transportation and extraction. Some specific applications include:

  1. Landfill venting:

    PU duct hoses help control odors and methane gas emissions from landfills.

  2. Ultraviolet (UV) dryers:

    PU duct hoses are utilized in UV dryers for air circulation and drying purposes.

  3. Pharmaceutical work:

    These hoses are employed in pharmaceutical facilities for air filtration and dust extraction.
Additionally, PU duct hoses are resistant to chemicals, moisture, wear, and tearing, making them suitable for various other applications.

3. Purpose: TRANSPORT MATERIALS

The purpose of a PU (Polyurethane) duct hose is to transport materials such as shavings, chips, granules, pellets, dry foodstuff, and
pharmaceutical preparations. PU duct hoses are lightweight, wear-resistant, flexible, and resistant to oils, solvents, and gas fumes.
They are often used in applications like lawn and leaf collection, dust control, and light bulk material handling. PU duct hoses are
suitable for various industries due to their ability to resist abrasion, tearing, punctures, degradation by hydrolysis and microbes,
and their wide temperature range (-40 to 200 degrees F). They are also crush-resistant, clear for viewing inside, and have a smooth
interior to ensure efficient airflow and prevent material build-up.
