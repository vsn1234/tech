CARBON FREE HOSE


1. Material: Smooth White Synthetic Rubber Tube

The material of Carbon Free Hose primarily consists of:
  1. Tube: Smooth White Synthetic Rubber Tube, which is non-conductive.
  2. Reinforcement: High Tensile Synthetic Plies.
These components are designed to provide resistance to heat,
ageing, abrasion, and ozone. The Carbon Free Hose is also available in different specifications
to cater to various piping needs.

2. APPLICATION :Transportation of power cables

The application of Carbon Free Hose primarily involves carrying power cables in industries where exposure to high temperatures,
current leakage, radiation, open flames, and hot metal splashes are common. These industries include glass works, electrical
furnaces, and iron & steel industries. The hose acts as a protective barrier for the cables, ensuring their safe operation
under harsh conditions. Additionally, Carbon Free Hose is used in conveying furnace coolant and carrying power cables in steel
mills and the glass industry, where non-conductive applications are required.

3. Purpose: Covering wires and cables in electrical and electronic devices

The Carbon Free Hose is a type of rubber hose designed for covering wires and cables in electrical and electronic devices.
It is specifically engineered to provide high flexibility and lightweight properties. These hoses are manufactured using,
high-grade raw materials, ensuring durability and reliability in various applications. The purpose of the Carbon Free
Hose can be summarized as follows:

  1. INSULATION

    The Carbon Free Hose is used to insulate and protect electrical wires and cables from external factors,
    such as heat, moisture, and physical damage.

  2. FLEXIBLITY

    Flexibility: The high flexibility of the Carbon Free Hose allows for easy installation and routing
    of wires and cables, even in confined spaces.

  3. TEMPERTAURE RESISTANCE

    The Carbon Free Hose can withstand a wide range of temperatures, typically from -10 to 150 degrees Celsius,
    making it suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.
