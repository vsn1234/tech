QUICK RELEASE COUPLING


1. Material: RESISTANT TO CORROSION

The material of quick release couplings can vary depending on the specific standard
or manufacturer. Common materials used in quick release couplings include
  • Stainless steel (AISI 316)
  • Carbon steel (EN-10277-3)
  • Brass (DIN-EN 12164)

2. Application: Versatile Application

Some Common Application are

  1. HYDRAULIC SYSTEM

    Quick release couplings are used in hydraulic systems for equipment maintenance or operation systems. They are designed
    according to different standards, such as ISO 7241-A, ISO 7241-B, Gromelle 6000, Hansen W6000, and Parker 60, which are subjected to
    different industry applications.

  2. TRANSPORATION:

    Quick release couplings are used for quick connection and disconnection of trailers with trucks through air brake lines,
    They are also widely used in coupling and decoupling rail carriages. Airplanes and ships use quick release couplings extensively.

  3. INDUSTRIAL APPLICATION:

    Nylon braided hoses are suitable for carrying gas, oil, diesel, and other fluids
    in automotive and racing applications. They are designed to withstand high temperatures and resist chafing.

3. Purpose:

QUCIK RELEASE COUPLING HAS VARIOUS PURPOSE SUCH AS
  1. Quick and easy connection or disconnection of fluid lines in hydraulic and pneumatic systems. To prevent fluid leakage during connection or disconnection.
  2. Facilitate equipment maintenance, operation, and fluid transfer without the need for tools.
  3. Applicable in various industries, including mining, construction, hydraulic jacks, and high-pressure fluid transfer systems.
  4. Designed for severe conditions where hydraulic shocks, system vibrations, or hose bending are possible.
